About ProApp

ProU Education's mission is to enable a smooth transition from education to professional life through global remote internships, tailored industry developed training curriculums and study abroad opportunities. ProU's team is made up of international counsellors and industry experts, who hand-built the programs to help adapt the next generation to fast-changing changing environments. In 2022, ProU aided over 2000+ students to become Pros by landing their dream careers and university, securing over $1 million in scholarships.
